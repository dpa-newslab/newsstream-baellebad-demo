About this work

It's just a demo to show how to do crossfilter-like visualizations with the great Vega visualization grammar.

Click on the bar chart to see how the circular marks get filtered and the table gets redrawn accordingly.

Another feature is the detail view - a Bootstrap modal that opens whenever you click on a data point. As you see, the browser can support handling arount 7000 data points, aggregating on the fly, but still being able to display every data point
